MY LIFE IN RUSSIA 
Student life in the Russia as a med strudent
Studying medicine in Russia offers a unique blend of academic rigor and vibrant student life. International medical students can expect a structured curriculum that includes both theoretical and practical training, with a focus on hands-on experience in hospitals. The medium of instruction is often English, but proficiency in Russian is essential for clinical rotations.
Accommodation options range from university-hostels to private apartments, with shared apartments being a popular choice for budget-conscious students. Public transport is widely available, including metro, buses, and ride-sharing services like Yandex.Taxi and Uber.
Cultural immersion is a significant aspect of the student experience, with opportunities to explore Russia's art, architecture, and cuisine. The Russian winter offers a chance to enjoy the country's natural beauty, while the summer allows for outdoor exploration.
Overall, studying medicine in Russia provides a rewarding educational experience with the added benefits of a rich cultural environment and a supportive community of international peers
